Day 1 in Sarawak

{ Day 1 : At KLIA }

We went to Sarawak on 14th February 2020.
Departure time was at 7:45 A.M
but we arrived at KLIA2 an hour earlier
than the departure time.

{ Day 1 : At KIA }

We have arrived at Kuching International Airport
at 9:20 A.M.

{ Day 1 : At Bako National Park }

Our first destination was Bako National Park.
We arrived at the park at 11:25 A.M.
Before arriving at the park,
we had to get on the boat in order to
get to the park.

{ Day 1 : At Bako National Park }

The journey to the park took about an hour.
It was so boring and fascinating at the same time,
because there are many natural boulders.
Bako National Park is a national park in Kuching Division, Sarawak, Malaysia. Established in 1957, it is the oldest national park in Sarawak..

{ Day 1 : At Bako National Park }

Apart of its famous place for wild animals, the park is
also famous for its jungle walking. There are 16 trails.
Since there are not many animals show up,
we decided to do jungle walking.
We tried to walk for about 50 minutes,
and gave up to continue another jungle walking.

{ Day 1 : At Bako National Park }

During our walk to the center, we found a mini cave.
And we decided to take a picture together infront of it.
While we were around at the cave,
we accidentally met with a boar.
It was wandering around the beach by itself.

Day 2 in Sarawak

{ Day 2 : At Kampung Budaya Sarawak }

After resting well for a night on first day,
Our first destination for second day was Kampung Budaya Sarawak.
The entrance fee is around RM85 for one adult.
There are many beautiful traditional houses.
It is so fascinating to see there are many kinds
of houses that can be made from trees and woods.

{ Day 2 : At The Brooke Gallery at Fort Margherita }

The second destination was
The Brooke Gallery at Fort Margherita.
I acknowledged so many histrorical events
that have happened in Sarawak.

{ Day 2 : At Darul Hana Bridge }

At night, our third destination was
Jambatan Darul Hana in Kuching.
This is the best location for me,
because there is musical fountain.
Musical fountain means the water fountain
will come out according to the music
It was so exciting for me, because it heals me
after having long walks for two days.

Day 3 in Sarawak

{ Day 3 : At Pasar Serikin }

We went to Pasar Serikin on third day.
Pasar Serikin was located at Bau, Sarawak.
It takes about 30 minutes to get there from our hotel.
There are many clothes made from batik,
and furnitures made from rattan

{ Day 3 : At Tasik Biru }

The next destination was at Tasik Biru.
The lake is very clear and emerald-like.

{ Day 3 : At Jong's Crocodile Farm and Zoo }

Last destination we went is Jong's Crocodile Farm and Zoo
There are many kinds of crocodiles.
The place also includes with other animals too,
such as birds, monkeys, deers, boars, fishes, and others.
